90 Day Fiance's Big Ed Reveals New Relationship While Mourning His Liz Woods Break Up

September 2024 · 6 minute read

Highlights

  • Big Ed is dating someone new after breaking up with Liz Woods, revealing his new girlfriend is not interested in his fame.
  • Big Ed has a rule to only date women with a "3" in front of their age, hoping to find Mrs. Right.
  • Liz Woods learned Big Ed canceled their wedding through a text from their officiant, leaving her devastated.

Since 90 Day Fiancé premiered in 2014, fans have been surprised many times. For example, fans didn't expect Chantel Everett and Pedro Jimeno's divorce and they were surprised to learn Russ And Paola Mayfield almost didn't join the show's cast.

On the other hand, even though some toxic 90 Day Fiancé couples stay together, nothing is surprising about Big Ed Brown and Liz Woods breaking up again. However, considering how often the couple have split up and reconciled, it was surprising when Big Ed revealed he had a new girlfriend.

This article will reveal what Big Ed said about breaking up with Liz Woods and dating someone new. Then the article will look at the heartless way that Woods learned their marriage had been called off.

90 Day Fiancé's Big Ed Is Dating Someone New Amid His Breakup From Liz Woods

After Big Ed Brown and Liz Woods' relationship had been tumultuous for a long time, the couple got into a fight ostensibly over taco pasta. Since Big Ed made taco pasta that was too spicy for Woods' daughter, that spawned a fight between the couple that led to their split.

In May 2024, Big Ed appeared on Page Six's "Virtual Reali-Tea" podcast to discuss his and Woods' latest breakup. During the resulting conversation, Big Ed revealed he was dating someone new. Big Ed also revealed that his new girlfriend is someone he shared a tight bond with.

“She’s a really good friend and we have very intimate conversations. She’ll tear up and I’ll tear up."

Of course, when someone famous starts dating someone new, observers are always going to have questions. After all, there is no doubt that there are a lot of people who'd love to date someone famous for attention, even if they don't actually like the person.

During Big Ed Brown's appearance on Page Six's "Virtual Reali-Tea" podcast, he claimed many people would love to date him. However, Big Ed explained that finding a good partner is difficult. “I have a lot of people that pursue me that want that and that honestly is the hardest part for me to be able to meet somebody.”

However, according to what Big Ed said during the podcast appearance, the new woman in his life isn't interested in his fame.

"And she wants nothing to do with my fame or whatever. She’s not any of that.”

In general, Big Ed didn't reveal very much about who the new woman in his life was. However, the 90 Day Fiancé star did reveal how old his new girlfriend was as he explained that he had a rule about who he would date in the future.

“The new rule is … I can’t date anyone without a 3 in front of it. So this one, she’s 38 and she’s a four-alarm fire! Maybe I’ll meet not Mrs. Right Now, but Mrs. Right. That’s my dream.”

Even though Big Ed has moved on to a new relationship, that doesn't mean his relationship with Liz Woods ending was easy for him. In fact, Big Ed told the hosts of Page Six's "Virtual Reali-Tea" podcast how he coped after his breakups with Woods.

As Big Ed explained, he dealt with his breakups with Woods by ridding himself of anything that would remind him of her.

"After every single breakup I would go through, and anything that I had of Liz, photographs, I would erase because I didn't want anything to remind me of her."

"That's part of Ed and Liz's history. And that's what I'm going to miss most."

During Big Ed's appearance on Page Six's "Virtual Reali-Tea" podcast, he also explained that he wasn't the only one who had moved on. According to Big Ed, Liz Woods was dating someone new. To Big Ed's credit, he said that even though it is difficult to watch, he wanted the best for Woods' new relationship.

“We did the fluffing of the chests when we met. He’s super good-looking. He’s young and I want them to be happy. Of course, it stung because that kind of happened right away. And Liz is young. That’s part of being young, this generation, where they kind of have to go from one relationship to the other.”

Liz Woods Learned Big Ed Canceled Their Wedding In A Heartbreaking Way

In modern society, many people have debated what people owe each other when they are breaking up. Most notably, some people think it is fine to break up with someone through a phone call or even via text, and others disagree.

As a Yahoo! News article that quoted her revealed, Woods didn't get a breakup phone call or a text. Instead, Woods says the couple had a fight,, she went to sleep, and Big Ed was "gone" when she woke up. Then, a third party's text informed Woods that Big Ed had canceled their wedding.

"I received a text message from our officiant telling me how sorry he was that the wedding was called off. Ed canceled our wedding without even telling me.”

Yahoo! News' article also detailed how Woods reacted in the aftermath of Big Ed unilaterally canceling their wedding.

During Big Ed's appearance on Page Six's "Virtual Reali-Tea" podcast, he claimed that Woods was likely still expecting them to reconcile. "I think Liz actually is waiting for me to call her up and say 'I'm sorry' like it's always been. I call, I apologize, OK, and we move on. And that's how the last 14 breakups have been."

Based on what Woods said, she didn't even really understand why Big Ed canceled their wedding. As a result, the only sense of the situation that Woods could make at the time was that Big Ed got cold feet.

“The only thing I can think of is Ed's getting cold feet and he never actually really wanted to get married because although we've been the best we've ever been, we still have our issues.”
