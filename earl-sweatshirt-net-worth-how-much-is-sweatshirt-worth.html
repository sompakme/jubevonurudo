Early Life and Career Beginnings

Earl Sweatshirt, whose real name is Thebe Neruda Kgositsile, was born on February 24, 1994, in Chicago, Illinois. He started rapping in seventh grade under the name Sly Tendencies and gained recognition when he joined the rap group Odd Future in 2009. At the age of 16, he released his debut mixtape “Earl,” which received critical acclaim.

“I started rapping when I was like twelve or something. My dad produced, so from like six I was super involved with music. But as far as rapping, I hadn’t been recording or taking it seriously at all. I would just play around with it and make little songs in seventh grade, but I didn’t start recording until like the summer going into my tenth grade year.”

– Earl Sweatshirt
