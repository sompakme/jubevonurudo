Celebrities will just about splurge on anything and everything, and cars are no exception. Having a luxurious car means you've made it, and there's nothing better than showing off your hot rod while driving down Hollywood Boulevard.

A lot of people are obsessed with cars and going to auto shows. However, if you're rich and famous, you don't just dream and obsess over these expensive rides; you go out and buy them whatever the cost. Stars like Jay Leno literally have a garage full of exotic and antique vehicles and his most expensive car is reportedly a Jay Leno's Rolls-Royce Silver Spur Limousine that's a whopping $14 million! It's not only the guys riding around in these luxury cars either. Have you seen Kylie Jenner's orange Lamborghini Aventador? The reality star dished out an insane $450,000 for her ride, and it's just one of many cars she owns. She also has a Ferrari Spider, Mercedes Maybach, and Rolls Royce.

But which celeb has the most expensive car sitting in their garage? You'll have to scroll through this list of twenty of the hottest and expensive vehicles owned by the rich and famous to see.

Lindsay Lohan's Maserati Quattroporte - $105,000

Lindsay Lohan may have had a weakness for a party-lifestyle, but she also has a soft spot for luxurious Italian sports cars. Lohan has been photographed many times driving around in a Maserati Quattroporte that has an estimated price tag of over $100,000. However, LiLo has had terrible luck with cars. She's been arrested numerous times for driving under the influence and had more car trouble when she had her assistant behind the wheel of her white Maserati and she collided with a Subaru in front of them. To top it all off, Lohan was also accused of hitting a woman with a stroller with her Maserati. Maybe Lohan should find a new brand of car to fall in love with.

Halle Berry's Aston Martin V8 Vantage Volante - $110,000

We all know Halle Berry from movies like Catwoman, Monster's Ball, and X-Men, and she's one of Hollywood's most adored actresses. The star also has a thing for classic cars, and her Aston Martin V8 Vantage Volante is a beauty when it comes to vehicles. The star was spotted by paparazzi leaving in her black Aston Martin after having lunch in West Hollywood, and we can't help but be a tad jealous. Obviously, riding around in anything with the name Aston Martin comes with a hefty price. It's been reported that the former Bond girl shelled out about $110,000 for her luxurious car.

Lady Gaga's Audi R8 GT - $162,000

You'd think that Lady Gaga would be driving something outrageous like a bright-colored car with unicorns detailed on it, but the extravagant singer is actually riding around in something way classier and sportier than you think. Not to disappoint all you Lady Gaga fans (or Monsters, as they are known), but the "Born This Way" singer is actually driving an Audi R8 GT. So the car isn't as flashy as we would think the singer would drive in, but hey, it's still a $162,000 hot rod, and she looks really good riding in the driver's seat of the silver car.

Kim Kardashian's Ferrari F430 - $186,925

You won't ever catch Kim Kardashian driving something that costs less than the price of a house nowadays. And even if you're still bewildered as to why she became so rich and famous, don't worry, we still are too. Regardless, the reality star has made millions by just being her, and her rides are always ridiculously expensive. Here the Keeping Up With the Kardashians star was pictured driving around in a Ferrari F430, which is priced at a whopping $186,925. The car can go up to a speed of 200 mph, and we aren't really sure why Kim needs such a fast car, but she sure does look good driving in it.

Paris Hilton's Custom Bentley GT Continental - $285,000

Are you surprised that Paris Hilton drives around in this all-pink custom Bentley GT Continental? We certainly aren't. Being a Hilton, the star probably received everything she wanted as a child, and now at the age of thirty-six, Paris is still living the good life and doing whatever the heck she wants to her cars even if it's throwing bright pink paint on her almost $300,000 luxury vehicle (we know there are a lot of people cringing right now). Let us also mention that every single part of the car is personalized with Hilton's very own signature, and there are pink leather seats and a pink dashboard, of course.

P.Diddy's Son, Justin's Maybach - $360,000

When we finally got our driver's licenses, our parents allowed us to take their cars to the mall or hang out with friends. Some of our parents even bought us our first car, and while it may not have been anything special, it was still a car. However, if you're Diddy's kid, you get the most expensive car when you turn sixteen. The rapper gifted his son with an insane $360,000 Maybach, but that wasn't all. Diddy threw his son a lavish sixteenth birthday party at a New York hotspot and, just in case Justin needed some cash, gave his son $10,000. Explaining why he gave his young son such a gift, he simply said, "I think it's appropriate to give my kids whatever I want to give my kids."

David Beckham's Rolls Royce Phantom Drophead - $407,000

David Beckham is one of the (if not THE) most well-known soccer player(s) in the world and certainly one of the wealthiest. Beckham is no stranger to having luxurious things, and one of his most precious items has to be this all-black Roll Royce Phantom Drophead that the soccer star bought in 2008. The car is estimated to cost a whopping $407,000. It was reported that David Beckham decided to get rid of the Rolls Royce and has since then bought numerous cars like the Porsche Turbo 911, Ferrari 612 Scaglietti, and a Jaguar XJ, just to name a few. Would you get rid of such a car if your name was David Beckham?

Kylie Jenner's Lamborghini Aventador - $450,000

Kylie Jenner is no stranger to buying expensive vehicles. One of her first cars was a Range Rover, and she was just sixteen years old. Last year, the reality star was photographed stepping out of her bright orange Lamborghini Aventador SV Roadster in Los Angeles. The car comes at an insane price of $450,000, and Jenner can easily take off from the annoying paparazzi from zero to 62mph in as quick as 2.9 seconds. Jenner's car collection is pretty impressive, to say the least. The star also has a Rolls Royce Ghost, a Ferrari 458 Spider, as well as a custom Range Rover and the latest Mercedes G-Wagon G550 4x4.

Jay Leno's Mercedes SLR McLaren - $500,000

Jay Leno cannot be beaten when it comes to collecting cars. The star literally had a television show based around the impressive cars in his gigantic car garage called Jay Leno's Garage. Leno owns approximately 286 vehicles (169 automobiles and 117 motorcycles), and we're pretty sure that number will just keep growing. One of his most luxurious rides in his garage is this Mercedes SLR McLaren that costs up to half a million dollars! In an episode of his show, Leno explained that the Mercedes was one of the best performance cars he's ever driven. The star also mentioned that the car comes close to matching the Porsche's Carrera GT around the ring.

Nicolas Cage's Ferrari Enzo - $670,000

Before having to let go of his sweet ride because of various financial troubles, actor Nicolas Cage was driving around in this $670,000 Ferrari Enzo in red. The car was first built in 2002 and caught many people's eye when it was featured in the film Charlie's Angels: Full Throttle, when actress Demi Moore was driving it on the beach. It is one impressive car with a top speed of 221 mph. Unfortunately, Cage had to sell his sports car to pay off his taxes and other debts with some analysts believing that the star would have gotten about a million for his car. Ouch...

Simon Cowell's Bugatti Veyron - $1.7 Million

Simon Cowell is another star that fell in love with the Bugatti Veyron. Anyone who loves a fast car wants this hot rod, but only a few people can actually shell out the $1.7 million for it. 50 Cent's Lamborghini Gallardo - $2 Million

The Lamborghini Gallardo is another vehicle that is mainly known for its speed (and its price tag, of course). The car is Lamborghini's best-selling model and is named after a famous breed of fighting bulls. Despite having filed for bankruptcy, 50 Cent managed to have one of the most expensive cars on the market. The rapper owns the LP550-2 Super Trofeo version of the car, and the vehicle is reportedly very rare since it was actually made for the Hong Kong market and had to be imported to the United States.

Floyd Mayweather's Koenigsegg CCXR Trevita - $4.8 Million

When Floyd Mayweather isn't kicking someone's butt in the boxing ring, you can find him among his insane collection of expensive and rare cars. One of his cars is the Koenigsegg CCXR Trevita, which he bought for $4.8 million. According to one report, the fact that the car hit the market is a huge deal because there were only two of these vehicles built. The "Trevita" name is Swedish for "three whites," which is in reference to the Koenigsegg's special diamond-weave, carbon-fiber bodywork. Mayweather put the car up on auction, and since only two exist, you can believe there were probably a lot of high rollers trying to purchase the rare vehicle.

Paul Pappalardo's Ferrari 250 GTO - $52 Million

The world's most expensive car, the 1963 Ferrari 250 GTO, was formerly owned by collector Paul Pappalardo. The collector, who isn't anyone necessarily famous, acquired the car in 1974, restored it, and even drove it in many historic races, including the 2002 Le Mans Classic. According to California based dealer Don Williams, the car is "like the Mona Lisa. It has a mystique. If you have a GTO, you have a great collection." An unidentified buyer in a private collection acquired the car. Whoever this lucky person is, he is the new owner of the most expensive car in the world going for an insane $52 million.
