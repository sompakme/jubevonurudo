Garrett Patrick Stubbs was born on May 26, 1993,  in Del Mar, California, to T. Patrick and Marti Jo. Stubbs is an American professional baseball catcher for the Houston Astros of Major League Baseball (MLB).

Due to Astros Catcher, Max Stassi being placed on a 10-day injury list, Stubbs was called up to the major leagues. It was on his 26th birthday, May 26, 2019. Stubbs made his major on May 28, 2019.

Stubbs wears jersey number 11 for the Houston Astros.
