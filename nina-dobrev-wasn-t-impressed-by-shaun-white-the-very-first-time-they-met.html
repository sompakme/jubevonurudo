Nina Dobrev And Shaun White Met At A Workshop

Nina Dobrev and Shaun West encountered each other for the first time at an award show in 2012, but it wasn't until 2019 that the two were formally introduced to each other. By early 2020 fans already started spotting the two stars together, and in late February 2020, the actress and the athlete posted separate photos from their South African safari. Fans quickly noticed that the two were at the same locations during the same time — even though they didn't post one photo together.
