The American fashion designer, businesswoman, and reality television personality, Ivanka Trump has a net worth of $300 million. She's the daughter of Donald Trump, who is also the President of the United States, and former model Ivana Trump. She became the senior advisor of her father as well as the first Jewish member of the First Family since converting to that religion before she married her Jewish husband, Jared Kushner.

This fourth-generation businessperson followed in her family's footsteps. She eventually became the executive vice president of the Trump Organization, as well as the boardroom judge of her dad's TV show, The Apprentice. She moved to Washington D.C. last January 2017, because of her husband getting appointed as the Senior Advisor to the President.

Last March 2017, she started to serve in her father's presidential administration. She assumed this position without a salary because of the public's concerns about her getting access to classified material without the restrictions of a federal employee. She is a part of the inner circle of the president before she became an official employee in his administration.
