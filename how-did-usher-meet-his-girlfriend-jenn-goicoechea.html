Usher and Jenn Goicoechea are a rare celebrity couple that has managed to keep much of their private life private, even while maintaining a presence on social media.

Therefore, it is not surprising that neither Goicoechea nor Usher have put little information out there regarding how they originally met. Because of this speculation has swirled since a December 2019 photo showed the couple backstage at the Rose Bowl getting close.

Here is how Usher met his girlfriend Jenn Goicoechea.

The Couple Has Known Each Other For Years

While the world believes based on the photos that they have seen that Usher and Goicoechea have only known one another for a short time, the two have been part of the same social circles for years.

After Usher and Goicoechea were first linked in December 2019, many believed that Goicoechea was a new lady in Usher's life. However, a picture that Goicoechea posted to Instagram in 2016 shows that the two have known one another for quite a bit longer.

Usher And Jenn Goicoechea Are Both In The Music Industry

The most obvious way that Usher and Goicoechea likely met one another is through mutual friends or acquaintances. And given that both have been a part of the music industry for quite some time, their paths have undoubtedly crossed a time or two in the past.
