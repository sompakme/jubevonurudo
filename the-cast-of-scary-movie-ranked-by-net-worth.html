The Cast Of 'Scary Movie' Ranked By Net Worth

August 2024 · 5 minute read

It's been over two decades since the iconic Scary Movie was released. And even after all this time, it is equally hilarious as it was back in 2000. Scary Movie — which spoofs other horror films such as Scream and I Know What You Did Last Summer —became an instant success and has grossed over $270 million worldwide on a $19 million budget.

Related: Here's How Anna Faris Landed Her Role In 'Scary Movie'

Today we take a look at the cast of the first installment of the Scary Movie franchise and we rank them according to their net worth. So, if you have ever wondered how much Anna Faris, Shannon Elizabeth, and Regina Hall are worth, you're in the right place. Keep on scrolling to find out just which of these actors lands at position #1.

10 Lochlyn Munro - $800,000

Kicking the list off is the Canadian actor, Lochlyn Munro, who played the role of Greg Phillippe. Apart from Scary Movie, Munro is also known for appearing in the 2004 comedy movie White Chicks, as well as TV series Charmed, and Riverdale. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Munro's estimated net worth is just below a million — $800,000.

9 Dave Sheridan - $1.6 Million

Landing at spot number nine of our list is Dave Sheridan, who portrayed the movie's antagonist, Doofy. Dave Sheridan — who worked as an intern at Saturday Night Live at the beginning of his career — has an estimated net worth of $1.6 million. His other work includes roles in Marlon Waynes' movies A Haunted House and Fifty Shades of Black. Sheridan also has his own rock band Van Stone, which he tours with.

8 Jon Abrahams - $1.7 Million

Next on the list is Jon Abrahams, who is mostly known for playing Scary Movie's secondary antagonist, Bobby Prinze. Abrahams has an estimated net worth of $1.7 million. Most of his net worth is derived from his roles in movies Meet The Parents, House of Wax, and Clover.

In 2016, Abrahams made his directorial debut with the drama movie All at Once, which follows the story of a young artist who decides to raise his friends' two daughters, after their tragic death in the 9/11 attack.

7 Cheri Oteri - $2 Million

Now let's move on to the American actress and comedian, Cheri Oteri, who lands at spot number seven, thanks to her $2 million net worth. Oteri's career started in 1995 when she was discovered by the producers of Saturday Night Live after she performed a monologue at comedy troupe The Groundlings in Los Angeles. After SNL, Oteri appeared in numerous movies like Inspector Gadget, Liar Liar, Shrek the Third, and Scary Movie, where she played Gail Hailstorm.

6 Regina Hall - $4 Million

Regina Hall played one of the most iconic roles of all comedy movies. Of course, we are talking about Brenda Meeks, Cindy's best friend in Scary Movie. Besides the Scary Movie franchise, Regina has appeared in numerous movies and TV series. Her most known movies are The Best Man, Think Like A Man, and Girls Trip. When it comes to television, she appeared in Ally McBeal, Law and Order: LA, and is currently starring in Showtime's series Black Monday. With an estimated net worth of $4 million, she is at position number six on our list.

5 Shannon Elizabeth - $7 Million

Opening up the top five is the actress and former model, Shannon Elizabeth, who you might recognize as Buffy Gilmore from Scary Movie. Her other acting gigs include appearing in comedy movies American Pie and Jay and Silent Bob Strike Back, as well as horror movies Thirteen Ghosts and Cursed. A part of her $7 million net worth comes from participating in poker tournaments.

4 Carmen Electra - $8 Million

Next on today's list is the actress, glamour model, and media personality Carmen Electra, who had a pretty small, but still iconic role of Drew Decker in Scary Movie. Carmen appeared in several other parody movies, including Date Movie and Meet the Spartans. Aside from her roles in parodies, Carmen Electra is probably most known for playing Lani McKenzie on NBC's iconic series, Baywatch. Her net worth is estimated at $8 million.

3 Shawn Wayans - $30 Million

Let's move on to the actor, writer, and producer, Shawn Wayans, who starred as Ray Wilkins in Scary Movie and its sequel. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Shawn's estimated net worth is $30 million. Most of it is derived from his acting gigs, but the fact that he co-writes and co-produces most of the movies he appears in surely brought in some extra money to his bank accounts.

2 Anna Faris - $30 Million

Aside from her work on the Scary Movie franchise — where she played the main role of Cindy Campbell – Anna Faris has appeared in numerous successful comedy movies such as Just Friends, My Super Ex-Girlfriend, and What's Your Number?.

Related: Here's How Anna Faris Spends Her $30 Million Net Worth

Faris also had a pretty good on television — she had a recurring role in the last season of Friends and she starred as Christy on the CBS sitcom, Mom. That is why it shouldn't come as a surprise that Faris ranks this high on our list, tying with Shawn Waynes.

1 Marlon Wayans - $40 Million

Wrapping the list up is the actor, writer, and producer, Marlon Wayans, who played the role of Shorty Meeks, Brenda's brother. Many people don't know that Marlon Wayans is actually one of the six writers of the first two Scary Movie installments. Marlon Wayans has also written and produced other comedy movies such as A Haunted Hause, Naked, and White Chicks. Marlon currently has a net worth of $40 million.
