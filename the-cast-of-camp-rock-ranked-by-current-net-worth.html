The Cast Of 'Camp Rock' Ranked By Current Net Worth

September 2024 · 5 minute read

“Camp Rock!” For readers who are fans of TikTok, you may have read that exclamation in the voices of the cast members after being reminded of the nostalgic movie. While this scene has become a trend on the social media app, Camp Rock is also a monumental movie for millennials that grew up watching Disney Channel.

Related: Disney Channel Stars Who Have Appeared In More Than One Disney Show

Camp Rock was released in 2008 and was the breakout role for Demi Lovato, as well as one of the first acting experiences for the Jonas Brothers. Between the less-than-perfect acting, the catchy tunes, and the stereotypical underdog-and-mean-girl story line, this movie was a favorite among Disney fans.

After over a decade, we’re curious… which cast member has the highest net worth today? The actors came to set with different levels of experience and have since gone in several different directions. Here is the cast of Camp Rock ranked by their current net worth.

9 Jasmine Richards (Peggy) - $500,000

Richards, who played one of Tess’s posse girls, has perhaps the lowest net worth of all the main cast members. She has been credited with 15 roles on her filmography, mostly acting as reoccurring characters in different television series. Jasmine quit acting in 2013 after being in the industry for a decade, leaving her net worth at an approximate $500,000.

8 Daniel Fathers (Brown) - $1.5 million

Daniel made his on-screen debut back in the year 2000 as a write-off character in the TV show Relic Hunter. Since then, he has gone on to book bigger and more frequent roles, spanning everything from shorts to television series to movies. Fathers is still acting and is credited for two shows set to be released in 2022, bringing his net worth to $1.5 million.

7 Alyson Stoner (Caitlyn) - $1.5 million

Perhaps one of the most surprising amounts on this list is for Alyson, who was reported to have a $1.5 million net worth. Stoner has not only amassed an astonishing 135 roles in her acting career but is also a professional dancer (having appeared in multiple Missy Elliot music videos as well as teaching at her own studio) and singer. She has most recently been in the sphere of voice acting for shows like Young Justice and Pete the Cat.

Related: What Happened To Disney Channel's Alyson Stoner?

6 Maria Canals-Barrera (Connie) - $2 million

Canals-Barrera has been on our screens since 1990. While at first it was just one-time roles here and there, she quickly got cast as characters that most millennials would recognize. As the voice of Sunset Boulevardez in Proud Family and Paulina in Danny Phantom to playing the non-magic mom in Wizards of Waverly Place, Maria has a current net worth of $2 million.

5 Meaghan Jette Martin (Tess) - $2.5 million

Meaghan had only started acting professionally a year before Camp Rock was released. Since then, she has been acting regularly in shorts and television shows, with occasional appearances in movies. Martin has continued acting through 2021, but it has mostly been in shorts and as a voice actor in different video games. These opportunities have given her a $2.5 million net worth.

4 Kevin Jonas (Jason) - $25 million

Kevin, who is most known for his part in the Jonas Brothers band, has actually been credited for a fair amount of work outside of his band’s music videos. Besides Camp Rock, he (along with his brothers) voiced the Cherubs in Night at the Museum: Battle of the Smithsonian, starred in JONAS with his siblings, and most recently appeared in an episode of the TV show Dash & Lily. All these accomplishments bring his net worth to $25 million.

3 Demi Lovato (Mitchie) - $40 million

Demi Lovato has grown up in the spotlight. From 2002 to 2004, they were one of the star children on the Barney & Friends series. After that, they continued acting in more television series until their debut in Camp Rock. Lovato discovered they had a future career in singing, and has since pursued that dream. Throughout their time in the industry thus far, they have reached a net worth of $40 million.

2 Joe Jonas (Shane) - $50 million

Joe, a third of the Jonas Brothers band, is tied with his brother Nick for the wealthiest cast member from Camp Rock. His resume looks similar to that of his brother Kevin’s, with one big exception: he continued a career in music after the band temporarily broke up in 2013. Getting a group together and titling themselves DNCE, this Jonas brother has a current net worth of $50 million.

1 Nick Jonas (Nate) - $50 million

As previously mentioned, Nick is tied with Joe for a net worth of $50 million. Just like his older bro, he also continued singing after the band’s initial split, except he went the route of having a solo career. Thanks to all the songs he’s produced, his Disney Channel television show, and his movie appearances, this Jonas is one of the wealthiest members of the Camp Rock team.
