The American psychological thriller film Deep Water is the film adaptation of the novel of the same name written by Patricia Highsmith. The movie is the latest addition to the novels of Patricia Highsmith which has been adapted to film including The Cry of The Owl, The Two Faces of January, Ripley Under Ground, A Kind of Murder and a lot more. Deep Water was advertised as an erotic film which is filled with infidelity which features the real life ex-lovers Ben Affleck and Ana de Armas.

The movie directed by the master of erotic thriller genre, Adrian Lyne, who brought out the best in all of the actors in the film. The union of Lyne, de Armas, and Affleck in the film is a winning combination from the start. Lyne was able to bring in a degree of psychological insight and solemnity in the film which is not usually present in that movie genre. The entire movie casts have given their roles justice, but aside from their acting talent, they are also sitting on some wealth they have built from their careers. Listed below are the richest cast of the movie, Deep Water.

Tracy Letts

American playwright, screenwriter, and actor Tracy Letts plays as Don Wilson in the latest Netflix film. The Tulsa born actor and playwright has been in the industry for decades now and has been a founding member of the Bang Bang Spontaneous Theatre. He has worked at the Steppenwolf Theatre Company for about 11 years. He currently has a net worth of $1 million.

Rachel Blanchard

Rachel Blanchard plays as Kristin Peterson in the movie. The Canadian actress who was born in Toronto first appeared on television in the series called The Kids of Degrassi Street in the year 1984. She has graduated from the Havergal College and Queen's University and has been active in the entertainment industry for years now. She currently has a net worth of $2 million.

Jacob Elordi

Australian actor Jacob Elordi portrays the role of Charlie De Lisle in the film. He plays as the friend and the piano teacher of Melinda Van Allen. Jacob Elordi is best known for his portrayal of Noah Flynn in the teenage Netflix movie The Kissing Booth. The critically acclaimed film has given Elordi exposure and made a name for him in the industry. He has always been fascinated with acting and at a very young age, he has been interested in the world of entertainment. He currently has a net worth of $3 million.

Finn Wittrock

Finn Wittrock is among the cast of the film playing as Tony Cameron. The American actor and screenwriter Finn Wittrock was born on October 28, 1984 in Lenox, Massachusetts. Before entering the world of entertainment, he studied at Los Angeles County High School for the Arts and The Juilliard School. He has starred in the soap opera titled All My Children for two years from 2009 until 2011. He initially began his career appearing as guest roles on numerous TV shows until he made his movie debut in 2004 in the movie Halloweentown. He currently has a net worth of $5 million.

Kristen Connolly

Kristen Connolly is also part of the movie playing as Kelly Wilson. The American actress was born on July of 1980 in Montclair, New Jersey. Among her most notable roles include Christina Gallagher in the Netflix series House of Cards and Dana in The Cabin in the Woods. She has been active in the industry for years now and has a current net worth of $5 million.

Ana de Armas

Cuban-Spanish actress Ana de Armas plays as Melinda Van Allen, the other half of the married couple who has fallen out of love and began playing some deadly mind games against each other. Their games started off as fighting against each other until they have begun seeing those around starts dying. The actress playing Melinda Van Allen, Ana De Armas, has been acting since she was a teen in Cuba before moving to Spain to further pursue acting. She has been in the industry for decades now and has amassed a net worth of $6 million.

Dash Mihok

American actor and director Dash Mihok plays as Jonas Fernandez in the film. He is best known for his portrayal of the character Brendan Donovan in the drama Ray Donovan. He has been in the drama Ray Donovan since 2013 which is why it is not at all surprising that he has accumulated a net worth of $10 million.

Ben Affleck

American actor, director, screenwriter and producer Ben Affleck plays as Vic Vac Allen who just allows his wife to have multiple affairs so that they can avoid divorce. He became the prime suspect after the lovers of his wife begin disappearing one by one. Vic Vac Allen is different from the actor playing him, Ben Affleck, since he has been divorced for years now. Ben Affleck has a massive net worth of $150 million thanks to his decades spanning career.
