Normal and regular kids are provided with necessities by their parents but have to earn other rewards. That means getting good grades, performing household tasks, achieving something in sports or winning competitions. Your parents will not just give you an item that is not really a necessity without you working hard for it. To do otherwise may just spoil you rotten.

And then, there are the kids of famous people that can just about get anything they want simply because they have rich and famous parents. By just being themselves and without the need to achieve something substantial, they are gifted with toys that normal people could only dream of. Here is a list of the top 10 most spoiled children of celebrities.

 

10. Suri Cruise - $2,000 Dolce & Gabbana trenchcoat

 

Suri Cruise is as cute as a button, having inherited all the great features of her parents, Tom Cruise and Katie Holmes. Fortunately for her, she is bound to inherit more than her parents’ looks, especially given the fact that her dad is one of the highest earning celebrities in the business. Let’s hope that intense paparazzi scrutiny will not make her a poor little rich girl. In the meantime, she can go about her life wearing Dolce & Gabbana trench coats that she was spotted wearing while touring Disney World’s Magic Kingdom.

 

9. Emme Anthony - $2,400 Chanel accessories

 

Emme Anthony has the potential of being one of the most famous Latinas in the world. She is the daughter of two of the Hispanic’s world most famous products, Marc Anthony and Jennifer Lopez. Her parents have divorced so Emme may probably benefit if the two decides to impress her with toys and gifts. She has already been spotted in high-end events like the Paris Fashion Week, where she was outfitted in Chanel accessories, including a bag worth more than $2,000 and a brooch that cost more than $300.

 

8. Blue Ivy Carter - $5,200 Swarovski-encrusted bathtub

 

Blue Ivy Carter is the baby daughter of Jay-Z and Beyoncé, two of the most powerful musicians today. At such a young age, she already has experienced luxury, courtesy of a bathtub that is encrusted with Swarovski crystals. After she bathes, she can then rests on her crib worth $3,500.

 

7. Haji Abdul Azim - $113,000 birthday flowers

 

Haji Abdul Azim is the Prince of Brunei, a small country that have been blessed with an abundant reserve of oil and natural gas. For his 25th birthday, he gave out to all his visitors a goodie bag that contained an iPod, some diamond jewelry and expensive Crème de la Mer facial creams. For his 30th birthday, he had several celebrity guests in attendance in a party bedecked with flowers that cost $113,000.

 

6. Justin Combs - $360,000 Maybach

 

His dad, Sean Combs, or P Diddy, is one of the richest hip-hop artists around. It just follows that the son, Justin, will get to benefit from all the hard work that his father did. For his 16th birthday, while other normal kids are pestering their parents for a chance to borrow and take out the family car, Justin was gifted with his very own Maybach luxury vehicle. Still, Justin can say that he worked hard for it because he does star in the football field. He showed enough talent to earn a scholarship to UCLA.

 

5. Liesel Pritzker - $8 million in Manhattan real estate

 

Liesel Pritzker is the heiress to the Hyatt hotel chain. In 2002, she and her brother received more than half a billion dollars after suing their family. Pritzker has a good head attached to her shoulders, having attended Columbia University.

She put the money to good use by buying an Upper West Side apartment for $6.2 million and a condo unit near her school for $2.29 million.

 

4. Valentina Paloma Pinault - $12 million estate in Los Angeles

 

Valentina Paloma Pinault, though still a kid, already has the perfect combination of wealth and beauty. The wealth part is provided by her father, Francois-Henri Pinault, the Chief Executive Officer of PPR, a company engaged in luxury goods. The beauty part comes from her mother, Salma Hayek, the busty Mexican goddess acclaimed for both her acting and smoldering looks. Valentina already vacations in St. Barts, the playground of the rich and famous. She also has a $12 million estate being held in trust until she becomes an adult.

 

3. Ekaterina Rybolovleva - $88 million apartment in Manhattan

 

Ekaterina Rybolovleva is the daughter of Russian billionaire Dmitry Rybolovlev, the potash tycoon that also owns the AS Monaco football club.

He already gifted her with homes in Switzerland and Monaco. He also bought for her the most expensive apartment in Manhattan.

 

2. Tamara and Petra Ecclestone - $155 million real estate in Los Angeles and London

 

Tamara and Petra Ecclestone are the daughters of Bernie Ecclestone, the Chief Executive Officer of the lucrative Formula One series. They are stunning beauties in their own right, with pretty faces attached to their sexy bodies.

To further add to their appeal are the tons of money that the two have at their disposal. While guys can readily fall for anyone with the deadly combination that the Ecclestone sisters possess, you should be wary about the spending habits of Tamara and Petra. The two redefines the meaning of high maintenance, as they once spent $155 million on a whim, buying a mansion in Los Angeles worth $85 million and a home across Kensington Palace in London just because they felt like doing it.

 

1. Sheikh Hamdan bin Mohammed bin Rashid al Maktoum - $300 million yacht

 

When you’re a sheikh and heir to a kingdom endowed with oil and natural gas, the cost of luxury items is just a drop in the bucket. Sheikh Hamdan bin Mohammed bin Rashid al Maktoum drives around in a Mercedes Benz, which is enough for a kid to be called spoiled rotten. The car costs $4.8 million because of its mink finish and finishing studded with Swarovski crystals. Oh, he also owns the world’s largest yacht worth $300 million.
