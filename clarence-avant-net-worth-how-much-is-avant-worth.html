The Life and Career of Clarence Avant

Clarence Avant, born on February 25, 1931, in Climax, North Carolina, is a legendary figure in the music industry. Known as the Godfather of Black Music, Avant’s career spans decades and is marked by remarkable achievements and contributions.

Avant began his journey in the music industry as a manager for Teddy P’s Lounge. From there, he went on to manage various performers and worked at esteemed record labels like Venture Records and Sussex Records. Avant’s entrepreneurial spirit led him to establish his own record label, Tabu Records. Throughout his career, Avant held various positions in record labels and production companies, leaving an indelible mark on the industry.

Avant’s accomplishments and dedication have solidified his status as a pioneer and visionary.
