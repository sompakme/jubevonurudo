Orlando Arcia is a professional baseball player who has become a household name in the sport. He is a talented shortstop with an impressive track record of success on the field. Arcia was born on August 4, 1994, in Anaco, Venezuela, and grew up playing baseball in his hometown.

Arcia began his professional baseball career in 2010 when he signed with the Milwaukee Brewers as an international free agent. He spent several years in the minor leagues before making his major league debut in August 2016. During his rookie season, Arcia played in 55 games and had a batting average of .219. He also hit four home runs and had 17 RBIs.
