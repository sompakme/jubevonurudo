Former president and CEO of the Internet services company Yahoo!, Carol Ann Bartz, has an estimated net worth of $100 million.

She was previously chairman, president, and CEO at Autodesk, the world's largest producer of design software for use in architecture, engineering and building construction. After having spent 14 years as CEO at Autodesk, the Wisconsin-graduate was named President and CEO of Yahoo! in January 2009. On September 6, 2011, Bartz was removed from her position at Yahoo! (via phone call), and CFO Tim Morse was named as Interim CEO of the company.
