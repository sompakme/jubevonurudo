Gigi Hadid Stays Silent On Zayn Maliks Health After He Gives Update On Foot Injury

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Highlights

  • Zayn Malik reassures fans that his foot is fine after being run over at Paris Fashion Week, sharing images of his scuffed but unbroken shoe on Instagram.
  • Gigi Hadid remains silent on Zayn's health crisis, focusing on their daughter Khai and sharing photos of her playing in the snow.
  • Zayn and Gigi's relationship has been tumultuous, with a reconciliation, the birth of their daughter, and then a breakup following an incident with Gigi's mother. Gigi is now dating Bradley Cooper.

Zayn Malik offered fans a health update after injuring his foot at Paris Fashion Week, but his ex-girlfriend Gigi Hadid is staying silent amid the health crisis.

Earlier this month, footage went viral on X (formerly Twitter) showing the former One Direction member appearing to get his foot run over while escaping a crowd in Paris, France.

Footage Shows Zayn’s Foot Getting Run Over By A Car

In the video, Zayn can be seen being ushered through the people by his bodyguards. At one point, a slow-moving car appears to run over his foot as the crowd made it hard for Zayn to maneuver. The singer kept a calm look on his face throughout the encounter, though he appeared to be slightly limping before his team was able to escort him to his waiting vehicle.

Many fans questioned online whether Zayn was okay following the incident, leading the crooner to offer a brief but reassuring update online.

“@kenzo @nigo Thanks for a great show. My foot is fine!! Thanks to my incredibly well made shoes,” Zayn wrote on Instagram alongside a carousel of images.

The first photo showed a side image of Zayn in a blue and black patterned jacket, presumable from the night he attended Kenzo’s fashion show and had his foot run over. The second image was a picture from the show itself, while the third photo was a close-up of Zayn’s foot following the accident.

Though his while sneaker was scuffed, the material wasn’t even broken, seeming to suggest Zayn suffered no serious injuries during the commotion (that and the fact he referred to his foot as “fine” in the caption).

Where Gigi Hadid And Zayn Malik Currently Stand

Though fans were freaked out about Zayn momentarily following the release of the viral footage, one person who remain quiet was his ex-girlfriend Gigi Hadid, whom he shares 3-year-old daughter Khai with.

While Zayn was in Paris, Gigi was seemingly in the U.S. with their daughter, as she shared a photo of the toddler playing in the snow on Instagram last Friday. One picture showed Khai dressed in a hot pink snow suit with a leopard print hat keeping her warm. The toddler’s face was turned from the camera; Zayn and Gigi are known for keeping their daughter’s identity hidden on social media.

Gigi and Zayn’s relationship has been a subject of public interest since they first started dating in late 2015. The high-profile couple, both well-known figures in the fashion and music industries, briefly separated in 2018 but reconciled in 2019. In September 2020, Gigi and Zayn celebrated the arrival of their first child, a daughter whose name they revealed as Khai in January 2021.

However, they broke up the following year after an alleged incident between Zayn and Gigi’s mother Yolando Hadid, for which he later plead no contest to charges from. Gigi is currently dating Bradley Cooper, while Zayn hasn’t been romantically linked to anyone recently.
