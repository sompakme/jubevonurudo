Espionage, action, intrigue ... and a whole lot of money; that is Matthew Vaughn's spy thriller, Argylle, featuring a top-notch cast and an immersive plot.

Henry Cavill, as the titular character, leads the ensemble with Bryce Dallas Howard, and they are joined by a plethora of household names, including a couple of Oscar winners, a major international pop star, a wrestling legend, the face of one of the greatest TV shows ever made, and the third highest-grossing actor of all time.

Despite a rocky initial opening and mixed reviews from critics, Argylle has made a splash at the box office; the opening weekend exceeded expectations, raking in $18 million in the United States and Canada alone.

Just three days after it premiered, Argylle grossed $18 million in the United States and Canada, and $17.3 million in other territories, for a total of $35.3 million worldwide.

With its production budget pegged at $200 million, analysts estimated that the film would need to make at least $500 million worldwide to make a profit for the studio.

From seasoned screen veterans to chart-topping music artists, the Argylle cast boasts a range of net worths that reflect their journeys in Hollywood.

Let's dive into the net worths of the cast of Argylle.

10 Ariana DeBose

Ariana DeBose's Net Worth (Estimated) - $4 Million

Ariana DeBose, a rising star in Hollywood, has garnered acclaim and accolades across various platforms, and her net worth is estimated at around $4 million.

Her journey began with a television debut on the series So You Think You Can Dance in 2009. DeBose was cast in her breakthrough role as Disco Donna in Summer: The Donna Summer Musical in late 2017.

Three years later, she graced the screen as cheerleader Alyssa Greene in Ryan Murphy's musical comedy The Prom, an adaptation of the eponymous Broadway hit.

It was in 2021 that DeBose reached new heights. She portrayed Anita in Steven Spielberg's adaptation of the iconic musical West Side Story.

The film catapulted her to stardom, earning her a Golden Globe, a SAG Award, a BAFTA, and notably, an Academy Award in the category of "Best Supporting Actress."

West Side Story grossed $76 million worldwide. DeBose has also appeared in the Apple TV+ musical comedy series Schmigadoon.

DeBose had her first acting role on a television series in 2016 when she played Sophia Ortiz in an episode of the CBS police procedural Blue Bloods.

9 Sofia Boutella

Sofia Boutella's Net Worth (Estimated) - Between $4 Million And $5 Million

Before taking up action roles in Hollywood, Sofia Boutella honed her skills on the international dance scene.

Her dynamic hip-hop and street style caught the eye of Nike in 2007, leading to her breakthrough as a dancer in their "Keep Up" campaign. This collaboration highlighted her talent and fierce femininity and paved the way for work with renowned artists like Madonna and Rihanna.

Boutella's film debut came in 2012's StreetDance 2, followed by her breakout Hollywood role in Kingsman: The Secret Service. The film grossed $414.4 million worldwide.

The Algerian-born actress brought her action prowess to the table in films such as Star Trek Beyond,Atomic Blonde, and The Mummy, alongside Hollywood heavyweights like Charlize Theron, Tom Cruise, and Russell Crowe.

Since 2018, Boutella has taken on even more central roles, starring in Gaspar Noé's dark thriller Climax and the future-noir action film Hotel Artemis.

In November 2021, Boutella was cast as the lead in the sci-fi adventure film Rebel Moon, directed by Zack Snyder for Netflix.

With many other projects like The Killer's Game lined up, there is no doubt that Boutella's estimated net worth of $4 million to $5 million will increase.

8 Catherine O'Hara

Catherine O'Hara's Net Worth (Estimated) - Between $10 Million And $12 Million

Catherine O'Hara has earned a net worth of between $10 million and $12 million in her five decades in the industry.

Her presence dates back to the original cast of Second City Television (SCTV) from 1976 to 1984, where she showcased her comedic genius. In 1990, O'Hara portrayed Texie Garcia in Dick Tracy, a box office success grossing $162.7 million.

The same year, she took on the role of Kate McCallister in Home Alone, followed by the 1992 sequel, Home Alone 2: Lost In New York, both earning immense global success with $476.7 million and $359 million in box office revenue, respectively.

Her other appearances include the mockumentary film Waiting For Guffman, written and directed by Christopher Guest.

She also appeared as Dr. Georgina Orwell in the first season of the Netflix black comedy-drama series A Series Of Unfortunate Events, which premiered in 2017.

O'Hara has more than 50 acting credits to her name, and her films have collectively grossed over $3.5 billion at the box office.

7 Bryce Dallas Howard

Bryce Dallas Howard's Net Worth (Estimated) - $25 Million

Born into Hollywood royalty as the daughter of director Ron Howard, Bryce Dallas Howard carved her own path to success, earning an estimated net worth of $25 million.

Her early roles were largely uncredited cameos in her father's films, but her breakout arrived in 2004 with M. Night Shyamalan's The Village. While the film met mixed reviews, it grossed $256.7 million worldwide against a $60 million production budget.

The turning point came in 2015 with her starring role in Jurassic World, a reboot of the iconic dinosaur franchise. The film smashed box office records, grossing over $1.6 billion, becoming her highest-grossing film.

While co-starring with Chris Pratt in the trilogy, a 2018 Variety report revealed a gender pay gap, with Howard earning $8 million for Fallen Kingdom compared to Pratt's $10 million.

Howard's career has continued to flourish. She headlined diverse projects like the heartwarming Pete's Dragon, the gritty crime drama Gold, and the Elton John biopic Rocketman.

Her directing endeavors, including episodes of The Mandalorian, further added to her diverse portfolio.

6 Sam Rockwell

Sam Rockwell's Net Worth (Estimated) - $30 Million

With a net worth of $30 million, Sam Rockwell's career trajectory proves that versatility pays off.

His first film role came in 1985, but it wasn't until his portrayal of the complex William "Wild Bill" Wharton in Stephen King's The Green Mile (1999) that he gained wider recognition.

The film's box office success of $286.8 million likely provided a significant financial boost. Despite lacking the traditional leading man mold, Rockwell carved his niche with riveting character portrayals.

He transitioned seamlessly between independent films like Matchstick Men and blockbusters like Iron Man 2, reportedly earning $500,000 for his role as Justin Hammer.

His dedication to his craft culminated in critical acclaim. He won the Academy Award in the category of "Best Supporting Actor" for his chilling performance as a racist cop in Three Billboards Outside Ebbing, Missouri (2017).

Another Oscar nomination followed for his portrayal of George W. Bush in Vice (2018).

These accolades and a diverse filmography, including Galaxy Quest and Moon, established him as a Hollywood heavyweight.

5 Bryan Cranston

Bryan Cranston's Net Worth (Estimated) - $30 Million

Bryan Cranston's estimated $30 million net worth reflects his successful career in Tinseltown.

The Oscar-nominated actor cut his teeth in local theater and scored early TV roles, including soap opera stints in the 80s. He landed a recurring role on Seinfeld before finding mainstream success as Hal, the wacky dad in Malcolm in the Middle.

However, it was his portrayal of Walter White in Breaking Bad that truly helped propel Cranston to superstardom.

His transformation into the morally ambiguous meth kingpin Heisenberg earned him widespread acclaim and awards recognition, including four Emmys for "Outstanding Lead Actor In A Drama Series," with his salary peaking at a reported $225,000 per episode – translating to millions per season.

Cranston co-developed and occasionally appeared in the crime drama series Sneaky Pete (2015 to 2019) and has also starred in the drama series Your Honor.

He has also voiced roles in Kung Fu Panda 3, and Isle of Dogs, Madagascar 3: Europe's Most Wanted, which grossed over $747 million, making it his highest-grossing film.

Cranston has appeared in over 34 films in his 40 years as an actor; his movies have collectively earned up to $3.7 billion at the box office.

4 Dua Lipa

Dua Lipa's Net Worth (Estimated) - $35 Million

Dua Lipa's meteoric rise in the music industry has launched her to pop icon status, with an estimated net worth of $35 million to match.

But beyond chart-topping hits and sold-out arenas, Lipa is venturing into a new realm: acting. Lipa's musical journey began in 2015 with her self-titled debut album, garnering critical acclaim and launching her into the spotlight.

Hits like "New Love" and "IDGAF" established her signature disco-pop sound, while collaborations with Calvin Harris and Blackpink complemented her global reach. Recently, Lipa took her talents to the silver screen.

While Argylle marks her official film debut, keen-eyed fans might recognize her brief cameo as Mermaid Barbie in Greta Gerwig's Barbie.

Lipa leverages her influence through lucrative brand partnerships with Yves Saint Laurent (representing their Libre fragrance), Evian, and Puma.

Her ambitious Future Nostalgia Tour grossed over $40 million in North America alone.

3 Henry Cavill

Henry Cavill's Net Worth (Estimated) - $50 Million

With a career packed with blockbusters and small-screen hits, it's no wonder Henry Cavill's net worth sits comfortably at an estimated $50 million.

His acting journey began in the early 2000s with roles in adaptations like The Count Of Monte Cristo and Capture The Castle. However, it was his portrayal of Charles Brandon in Showtime's The Tudors that gained him international recognition.

The turning point came in 2011 when he donned the iconic cape and tights as Superman in Man Of Steel.

According to The Hollywood Reporter, Henry was reportedly paid $300,000 upfront for Man Of Steel, likely in addition to back-end bonuses based upon box office profits.

Man Of Steelgrossed $668 million worldwide, making it the highest-grossing solo Superman film of all time. Reprising his role in Batman v Superman: Dawn Of Justice and Justice League, Cavill's earnings likely climbed considerably.

Variety speculates Cavill probably made around $20 million from Justice League. Cavill showcased his range with high-profile projects like Mission: Impossible – Fallout, playing the villain opposite Tom Cruise.

He also starred as Geralt of Rivia in Netflix's The Witcher.Variety reported that Cavill was paid $400,000 per episode in the first season; the figure was bumped to $1 million per episode for season two.

Cavill appeared as Superman again in a post-credits cameo in Black Adam alongside Dwayne Johnson, which reportedly earned him $250,000.

2 John Cena

John Cena's Net Worth (Estimated) - $80 Million

John Cena's $80 million net worth reflects a unique trajectory, transitioning from professional wrestling titan to Hollywood heavyweight.

A record-holding 16-time WWE world champion, his charisma and in-ring prowess cemented his legend. But Cena's ambition extended beyond the squared circle.

During his time in WWE, Cena was one of the biggest stars in the industry and reportedly earned $8.5 million per year. His acting career began just two years after joining WWE, with a small role in Ready To Rumble.

The 2006 action film The Marine marked his first lead, grossing $25 million on a $15 million budget. By 2015, Cena had transitioned into a sought-after actor, landing roles in comedies like Trainwreck and Daddy's Home 2.

These films were box office hits, showcasing his comedic timing and broadening his audience.

A pivotal moment came in 2021 with The Suicide Squad, where he portrayed Peacemaker, a role that brought him a different brand of audience and earned him $7 million.

Cena reprised the role in a successful HBO spin-off series, which in turn established him as a leading man capable of carrying an action franchise.

1 Samuel L. Jackson

Samuel L. Jackson's Net Worth (Estimated) - $250 Million

With a net worth of $250 million, Samuel L. Jackson is not just a Hollywood icon, but also a box office powerhouse.

His films have collectively grossed over $28 billion worldwide, making him the highest-grossing actor of all time. Jackson made his professional theater debut in Mother Courage And Her Children in 1980.

Early film roles in projects like Coming To America and Juice laid the groundwork for his breakout performance in Quentin Tarantino's Pulp Fiction.

His role in the film earned him international recognition and an Academy Award nomination. Pulp Fiction grossed $213 million globally.

Jackson also became a fan favorite as Jedi Master Mace Windu in the Star Wars prequels, and as Nick Fury in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, appearing in eleven films to date.

Jackson's salary reflects his star power. Reports indicate his base salary for starring roles is $10 million, reaching $20 million with backend deals.

His recurring role as Nick Fury reportedly brought in between $4 million and $6 million per film (with bonuses), and his Capital One endorsement deal alone brings in an estimated $10 million annually, per The Hollywood Reporter.
