Zoe Saldaña's Journey To A $40 Million Net Worth

Zoe Saldaña's journey to a $40 million net worth started with her acting career. She made her film debut in the teen drama Center Stage in 2000, where she danced her way into our hearts. Her passion for acting was on point for attracting a fan base, and that is just what she did.

Her big breakthrough, though, came with the role of Neytiri in James Cameron's Avatar, which became the highest-grossing film of all time, earning her a massive amount of attention and a $4 million paycheck.

Saldaña continued to impress with her roles in major franchises, playing Gamora in the Guardians of the Galaxy series and Uhura in the Star Trek reboot. The success of Zoe Saldaña's films is a rarity in that, of the only six movies that have ever cleared $2 billion at the box office, she has been in four of them.

Zoe Saldaña's Husband, Marco Perego's $5 Million Net Worth

In contrast to Zoe Saldaña's net worth, her husband, Marco Perego's net worth is estimated at $5 million. Perego, an Italian artist, filmmaker, and former professional soccer player, has found success in his own right, but on a different scale.

Marco Perego was almost a professional soccer player. At a young age, Perego was invited to play on a soccer team in Venice. All was going well until a leg injury at the age of 21 forced him to retire. While he didn't reach the same heights of fame and fortune as some of his peers in the soccer world, his time as a player likely contributed to his overall net worth.
